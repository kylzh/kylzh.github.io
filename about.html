🧐 A little bit about me.

I'm Kayla Zhu (朱泳茹), a Vancouver-based data journalist and content writer interested in all things data journalism.

I grew up in East Vancouver before hopping over to Toronto to study journalism at Toronto Metropolitan University (and do a minor in visual communication, a.k.a graphic design).

I quickly fell in love with data journalism – everything from scraping websites to analyzing a huge spreadsheet to creating easy-to-understand, accessible data visualizations and maps. Anything involving telling compelling stories or explaining complicated concepts with data, I'm game.

Beyond data journalism, I've worked in a range of journalism/editorial roles as well as communications roles and have significant experience in multimedia, cross-platform communication using social media, email copy, graphic design and content writing. You can check out my work history on my LinkedIn.

💻 I'm open to freelance writing, design or data analysis/visualization assignments (or a combination of any of those things). Feel free to shoot me an email at kayla.yr.zhu@gmail.com if you're interested in working with me or want to chat about some of my previous work!
